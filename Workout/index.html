Workout

Repetitions: 3x20 bodyweight or 3x12 weighted

Squats

Notes

- Most folks don't go quite low enough with squats.

- As a rough guideline, the top of your thighs should be parallel with the ground.

- You should feel it in your butt at the end of the movement.

Planks

Notes

- Targets your hips and core

- Working out your core can increase the muscle tension

and pull in your midsection, similar to a girdle.

Glute Bridges

Notes

- I'll either do these as single leg bridges and then finish with a

double leg set or just use weight to make it harder.

- Focus on full extension at the top of the bridge for glute activation.

Banded Abductions

Notes

- This motion hits the outer aspects of the butt

(the Glute Medius and Minimus).

- If you're using exercise bands start with a moderate tension

and focus on full range of motion.

Lunges

Notes

- I like these because they're relatively easy to perform

and activate the glutes more than most exercises.

- I also find that walking lunges or step-ups onto an exercise bench

prevent me from cheating and not going low enough.

Step-ups

Notes

- Same as lunges
